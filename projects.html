Projects


Some of the projects I have worked on throughout my career.

Enterprise to Computer : Star Trek chatbot

The Enterprise to Computer bot (E2Cbot) was created as part of 'Deep Learning for Automated Discourse' at Penn. The premise was to add a personality to the chatbot which was hosted as an Amazon Alexa skill. E2Cbot captures the 'style' of Star Trek by incorporating references from the show along with the peculiar tones of the show's characters. Details can be found at the Github repository and at Arxiv. I collaborated with Mansi Vashisht and Abheek Basu under the guidance of João Sedoc and Professor Lyle Ungar for this project.

Computation of Renewable Energy Harvesting

Simulated the working of harvesters and examined neighbourly harvesting choices for residential areas using machine learning on historical data. I collaborated with Guy Gadola under the guidance of Professor Daniel Mosse for this project.

Face Morphing

I explored Computer Vision during my time at Penn and one of the projects I did was face morphing. I took a picture of mine and tried to morph it to look like the Bollywood and Hollywood actor Deepika Padukone. Please try a browser other than Chrome if the video doesn't play.
