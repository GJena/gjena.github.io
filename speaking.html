Public Speaking


Apart from mentoring and informal teaching, I have also ventured into public speaking. Below is a snapshot of the different events I have spoken at in the past few years.
If you are looking for women speakers, may I recommend this amazing list of more than 1500 women in Data Science and this wonderful resource of women scientists.

'Introduction to Data Science' for KeepCoding community

September 20, 2018

Will host a webinar on 'Introduction to Data Science' for the KeepCoding Madrid community. KeepCoding inspires people to join the field of technology and reduce unemployment by filling empty tech positions. The webinar is open to all and the link will be posted here soon.

'A Day in the Life of a Data Scientist' for IBM SWIFT community

September 14, 2018

Will be presenting on 'A Day in the Life of a Data Scientist' for the internal community SWIFT - Successful Women Inspiring Future Talent at IBM.

Pre-GHC meetup by AnitaB.org and Systers

September 13, 2018

Panelist for the AnitaB.org and Systers meetup organized in San Francisco as a pre-cursor to the Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing 2018. I shared my past experiences and tips on how to make the most of the Grace Hopper conference. You can view the recording here.

WiMLDS x PyLadies: Paths to Data Science

July 26, 2018

Panelist for WiMLDS (Women in Machine Learning and Data Science) and PyLadies meetup on different paths to data science.

'Data Science and You' at SFPL

March 31, 2018

Delivered a talk at San Francisco Public Library on how data science is shaping the world we live in - right from grocery shelves to social media like Facebook.

'Enterprise to Computer : Star Trek chatbot' at Women Talk Tech, Reddit

March 31, 2018

Presented my graduate school project at Women Talk Tech event at Reddit, San Francisco. The project was created as an Amazon Alexa skill for the purpose of giving a personality to a chatbot - that of a Star Trek fan. You can view the recording here.
