Teaching and Mentoring


I am passionate about exposing more people to the world of Computer Science and making them aware of the interesting, exciting and challenging problems that can be solved through it.

Guest speaker, Big Data Week webinar, KeepCoding

October 23, 2018

Webinar on Data Science as part of Big Data week for the KeepCoding Madrid community. KeepCoding inspires people to join the field of technology and reduce unemployment by filling empty tech positions. The webinar is open to all. Register for free here.

Guest instructor, Hack Reactor

September 20, 2018

Guest instructor for underrepresented groups in the Telegraph track for Hack Reactor software engineering bootcamp. The talk introduced basic and intermediate Python concepts using mini-projects to provide hands-on coding.

Mentor, AI4ALL Spring Fellowship

Early 2018

As a mentor for AI4ALL AI Spring Fellowship, I guided a group of high school girls for three months to use Natural Language Processing to parse 911 EMS calls and prioritize them. AI4ALL is a non-profit working to increase diversity and inclusion in AI.

Python Instructor, Program Your Future, University of California - Berkeley

August 2016

Taught a coding workshop to middle-school and high-school students as part of Program Your Future, University of California - Berkeley. The objective was to teach programming by implementing the Pong game in Python.

Guest Speaker, PyLadies Remote

March 2018

Delivered an Intro to Python talk for PyLadies remote for more than 60 attendees. The talk introduced foundational concepts of programming using Python. A recording of the session can be found here.

Guest Lecturer, San Francisco Public Library
