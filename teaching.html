Mentor for the non-profit AI4ALL

Early 2018

As a mentor for AI4ALL AI Spring Fellowship 2018, I guided a group of three high school girls for three months. Founded by Dr. Fei-Fei Li and Dr. Olga Russakovsky, AI4ALL is a non-profit working to increase diversity and inclusion in artificial intelligence. My mentees used Natural Language Processing to parse 911 Emergency Medical Services calls and prioritize them. You can read more about their project here.
