Creating sustainable impact through technology and advocacy


Data Scientist and International Speaker


I am a seasoned Data Scientist with over 7 years of industry experience. Currently, I am part of the Insights and Engagement team for IBM Software. I work at the interesting intersection of User Research and Design and use data to understand users' struggles and opportunities to enhance their experiences. As the sole Data Scientist in the organization, I support 100+ user researchers and designers. I have received the IBM Outstanding Technical Achievement Award (Major), one of the most prestigious company-wide awards as recognition of my work.

I live in San Francisco where I spend most of my time imploring and exploring datasets using Machine Learning. I'm interested in Natural Language Processing and am fascinated by human language. I graduated with a Masters in Computer Science from University of Pennsylvania.

I spend my time outside of work as a public speaker. Over the last few years, I have delivered 50+ talks and workshops across 10+ countries to over 2000 individuals. I taught Python at the San Francisco Public Library and also frequently mentor school and university students.

I am extremely passionate about encouraging, mentoring, and empowering people, especially women and students, in the world of technology. I strongly support exposing children early on to Computer Science and using the superpower of coding to solve exciting and meaningful problems.

I have been recognized by the Python Software Foundation as a Fellow for my extraordinary efforts and impact upon Python, the community, and the broader Python ecosystem. I have received multiple awards for my community work and advocacy. I serve on the AI/ML Advisory Board for DevNetwork. I am also an ambassador for the non-profit Women in Data Science, as well as a part of the leadership for the Golden Gate Section of the non-profit Society of Women Engineers. Outside of the world of bits and bytes, I like to write, cook, and explore new places.

I love being a part of something that delivers a positive change. If you are seeking collaboration to make a difference, let's connect and create something impactful together!
